About Glitch Shop

Our Mission

The Glitch Shop is a skate shop with soul, dedicated to promoting social change and supporting those who are doing the work.

We believe that everyone has the power to make a difference, and our mission is to help you find your voice and make an impact in the world.

Our Story

The Glitch Shop was founded in 2023 by a group of skaters who wanted to create a space for the skate community that went beyond just selling gear. They wanted to build a community that was focused on social change, empowerment, and support for those who needed it most.

From the beginning, the Glitch Shop has been about more than just skateboarding. It's a place where people can come together to learn, grow, and make a difference. Whether you're a seasoned skater or just starting out, you're welcome here.

Our Values

  • Social Change: We believe in using our platform to promote positive social change in our communities.
  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals to make a difference in their own lives and in the lives of others.
  • Inclusivity: We believe in creating a welcoming and inclusive space for everyone, regardless of race, gender, sexual orientation, or ability.
  • Community: We believe in building a strong and supportive community that lifts each other up and helps us all grow.
  • Sustainability: We believe in taking care of our planet and doing our part to reduce our impact on the environment.
